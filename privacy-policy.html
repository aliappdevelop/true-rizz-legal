Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 25, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how TrueRizz AI ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects the information of users ("you", "your") who use our mobile application ("TrueRizz AI"). By accessing or using the app, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use the app, including:

  • Anonymous device identifiers (Firebase-generated ID)
  • App usage data (feature access, timestamps, number of daily interactions)
  • Subscription status (free or Pro)
  • Error and crash logs (for app stability)

1.2 User-Generated Content

When you generate a reply using TrueRizz AI features like Rizz Generator or Rizz Coach, we store the reply content anonymously to improve user experience and provide you with history access.

1.3 Payment Information

Payments for Pro features are handled by Google Play or Apple App Store. We do not store or have access to your credit card or payment data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Track free vs Pro usage for feature access
  • Improve app performance and features
  • Provide access to your pickup line history (if Pro or within daily limit)
  • Prevent abuse of free usage limits
  • Diagnose issues and debug crashes

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We only share information with:

  • Firebase (authentication, database, and analytics)
  • OpenAI or other AI model APIs (to process your text prompts)
  • App store providers for payment processing (Google/Apple)

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data. All connections are encrypted using HTTPS, and data is stored securely in Firebase.

5. Data Retention

We retain your anonymous usage data and message history for up to 12 months unless otherwise required for security or legal reasons.

6. Your Rights

As a user, you can request deletion of your data or account by contacting us at truereizz.ai@gmail.com.

7. Children's Privacy

TrueRizz AI is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted within the app and on our GitHub Pages site.
