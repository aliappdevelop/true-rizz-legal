Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 12, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how TextMuse AI (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects information from users (“you”, “your”) of our mobile app. By using TextMuse AI, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Data

We automatically collect:

  • Anonymous device identifiers (Firebase-generated)
  • Usage metrics (features accessed, timestamps, daily interactions)
  • Your subscription status (Free or Pro)
  • Error and crash logs (to improve stability)

1.2 User-Generated Content

Replies you generate (pickup lines, smart replies) are stored anonymously so you can access your history and to improve our AI suggestions.

1.3 Payment Information

All payments are processed by Google Play or the Apple App Store. We do not collect or store payment credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Enforce free vs Pro limits
  • Improve performance and AI quality
  • Provide message history access
  • Prevent abuse of free features
  • Diagnose and fix errors

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. We share only with:

  • Firebase (authentication, storage, analytics)
  • AI service providers (e.g., OpenAI) for response generation
  • Google/Apple for payment processing

4. Data Security

We use HTTPS and encrypted storage. Access is restricted to authorised personnel.

5. Data Retention

We retain data for up to 12 months unless you request deletion sooner.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct or delete your data. To request deletion, see Section 7.

7. Deleting Your Data

To delete all data we hold about you, email us at truereizz.ai@gmail.com. Please include your registered email. We will erase your data within 30 days.

8. Children’s Privacy

TextMuse AI is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will notify you in-app and update the Effective Date. Continued use implies acceptance.
