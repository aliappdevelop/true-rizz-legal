Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 12, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how TextMuse AI (“we”, “our”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects information from users (“you”, “your”) who use our mobile application. By using TextMuse AI, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Data

We collect the following data automatically:

  • Anonymous device identifiers (generated via Firebase)
  • App usage data (features used, timestamps, daily limits)
  • Crash logs and performance diagnostics
  • Subscription status (Free or Pro)

1.2 User-Generated Content

Replies and pickup lines you generate using the app are stored anonymously to provide message history and improve our AI results.

1.3 Payment Information

All purchases are handled securely through Apple or Google. We do not collect or store credit card or billing details.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To deliver app features and enforce usage limits
  • To provide access to your chat and message history
  • To improve AI performance and overall app experience
  • To detect abuse of the free plan or automated use
  • To fix bugs and improve stability

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your data. Data is shared only with:

  • Google Firebase (for storage, crash reporting, and analytics)
  • OpenAI or similar services (to generate AI-based responses)
  • Apple and Google Play (for billing and subscription management)

4. Data Security

We use secure connections (HTTPS), encryption, and access controls to protect your data. Only authorised personnel may access stored data.

5. Data Retention

We retain data for up to 12 months for feature support, unless you request deletion earlier.

6. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your data at any time. Contact us via the method in Section 10.

7. Deleting Your Data

8. Children’s Privacy
