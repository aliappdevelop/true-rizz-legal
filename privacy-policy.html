Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 12, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how TextMuse AI (“we”, “our” or “us”) collects, uses, and protects information from users (“you” or “your”) who install or use our mobile app. By using TextMuse AI, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Automatically Collected Data

We automatically collect:

  • Anonymous device identifiers (Firebase-generated ID)
  • Usage metrics (features accessed, timestamps, daily interaction counts)
  • Your subscription status (Free or Pro)
  • Error and crash logs (to improve stability)

1.2 User-Generated Content

Replies you generate (pickup lines, smart replies) are stored anonymously for history access and to improve suggestions.

1.3 Payment Information

All payment processing is handled by Google Play or the Apple App Store. We do not store or access your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Enforce free vs. Pro limits
  • Improve performance and AI quality
  • Provide your message history
  • Prevent abuse of free features
  • Diagnose and fix app issues

3. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your data. We share only with:

  • Firebase (authentication, storage and analytics)
  • OpenAI or similar AI APIs (for text generation)
  • Google/Apple (for payment processing)

4. Data Security

We use HTTPS and encrypted storage to protect your information. Access is restricted to authorised personnel.

5. Data Retention

We retain data for up to 12 months unless you request deletion sooner.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your data. To exercise these rights, contact us.

7. Deleting Your Data

If you wish to delete all data we hold about you, please email us at truemuse.ai@gmail.com. Include your registered email address and we will erase your data within 30 days.

8. Children’s Privacy

TextMuse AI is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
